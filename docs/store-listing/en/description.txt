P-Trainer – Athlete Management App for Pilates Instructors

A professional mobile app designed for Pilates instructors to easily manage their athletes and studio operations.

🏋️ ATHLETE MANAGEMENT
• Create unlimited athlete profiles (Premium)
• Detailed profiles with name, package info, and registration date
• Active/Passive athlete separation for easy tracking
• Access athlete details with a single tap

📅 WEEKLY LESSON PLAN
• View your lesson schedule for each day of the week
• Instantly see which athlete has a lesson on which day and time
• Easily plan your daily lesson workload

📊 PERIOD TRACKING
• Create lesson periods for each athlete
• Clear tracking with start/end dates
• Lesson cancellation and makeup management
• View progress status in real-time

📏 BODY MEASUREMENTS
• Record chest, waist, and hip measurements
• Track progress with historical measurement data
• Show your athletes' development with concrete data

💰 PAYMENT TRACKING
• Record payment status per period
• Instantly view Paid / Pending statuses
• Simplify your financial tracking

🔐 SECURITY
• Password-protected login system
• Your data is securely stored on your device
• Only you can access personal data

🌍 MULTI-LANGUAGE SUPPORT
• Turkish, English, Spanish, and Dutch

⭐ PREMIUM FEATURES
The free version includes a limit of 3 athletes and 1 period per athlete. With Premium:
• Unlimited athlete profiles
• Unlimited period creation
• Body measurements module
• Weekly lesson plan
• Payment tracking
